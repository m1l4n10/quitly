# quitly - Quit your bad habits and fix yourself!

quitly is a simple and focused **web application** designed to help you break bad habits and regain control of your time. 
Unlike many traditional apps, quitly **runs directly in your browser**. This means **no app store downloads, no clutter** - just quick access and instant setup.
It’s built for anyone **who wants to eliminate negative habits** such as smoking, junk food consumption, or sugar addiction, and replace them with a healthier, more intentional lifestyle.


With the **“Export Data” feature**, you can securely save your data **without relying on server hosting or external storage** like GitHub.
**All your data is stored locally** - either in your **browser’s cache** or as a **.json file** on your device. This ensures **full control** and **privacy over your information**.

<hr>

You can easily add quitly to your **home screen** and use it like a native app:

1. Tap the **three-dot menu** in your browser.
2. Select **“Add to Home Screen”**.
3. Choose one of the following:
   **• Install → for a full app-like experience**
   **• Create Shortcut → for a simple link on your home screen**
5. Open quitly **from your home screen**.
6. *Start your timer or **import your existing data**.
